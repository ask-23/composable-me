# Engineering Manager, Asset Management (Enterprise Zone) - Zapier

**Position:** Engineering Manager, Asset Management (Enterprise Zone)
**Location:** Remote (USA and Canada)
**Type:** Full-time

## Position Overview
Lead a newly formed Asset Management team within Zapier's Enterprise Zone, responsible for building foundational platform infrastructure that enables enterprise customers to manage their automation ecosystems at scale.

## Key Responsibilities

### Team Leadership & Development
- Manage a team of 6-8 cross-functional engineers (frontend, full stack, backend)
- Conduct weekly one-on-ones and team meetings
- Mentor engineers in distributed systems, compliance-sensitive design, API design, and platform thinking
- Recruit, onboard, and train new engineering talent

### Product & Strategy
- Work with product and design partners to translate technical capabilities into business outcomes
- Lead platform strategy and articulate the value of long-term investments to skeptical stakeholders
- Balance foundational platform work with customer-facing feature delivery
- Drive platform adoption across the organization

### Technical Oversight
- Participate in code reviews and establish quality standards
- Monitor team health and performance metrics
- Focus on data handling, API contracts, and migration strategies
- Maintain customer empathy for both external enterprise users and internal product teams

### Core Focus Areas
The team will build systems enabling:
- Asset transfers and consolidation across accounts and workspaces
- Hierarchical organization, search, and metadata layers
- Compliance features (GDPR/CCPA compliance, data deletion, retention policies)
- Public and internal APIs for self-service and embedded management

## Required Qualifications
- Minimum 2 years managing cross-functional engineering teams
- Background with distributed systems and multi-tenant architectures
- Experience building foundational platforms, not just individual features
- Shipped production code with understanding of software development trade-offs
- Deep customer empathy regarding enterprise constraints
- Strong communication and stakeholder alignment abilities
- Platform thinking mentality and long-term leverage focus
- Comfort with compliance-sensitive design and data governance
