# Engineering Manager, AI Capabilities - Zapier

**Position:** Engineering Manager, AI Capabilities
**Location:** Remote (USA and Canada)
**Type:** Full-time

## Position Overview
Zapier seeks an Engineering Manager to lead the AI Capabilities team, responsible for building the company's AI platform with APIs and infrastructure supporting LLM and agent experiences across the organization.

## Key Responsibilities
- Lead 4-6 engineers with technical direction, mentorship, and career development
- Drive architectural decisions aligned with Zapier's vision and technical standards
- Cross-team collaboration to evaluate technical choices and remove execution blockers
- Code review and contribution when needed for bug fixes and prototypes
- Foster experimentation culture emphasizing rapid learning and continuous improvement
- Stakeholder communication through one-on-ones, team meetings, and leadership updates
- Problem identification and engineering process improvements

## Required Qualifications
- LLM product experience: hands-on experience building and shipping LLM features
- Platform building background: Developer tools, APIs, or infrastructure work
- Technical stack fluency: TypeScript, React, Node.js, Python, Django
- Engineering leadership: Production-level code quality and team development skills
- Communication strength: Balance technical debt against product concerns across disciplines
- Hands-on engagement: Regular pairing with engineers despite non-IC status
- Pragmatic mindset: Balance architecture planning with execution speed
- Startup mentality: Comfortable with ambiguity and multiple responsibilities

## Desired Traits
- Autonomous agents development experience
- User-first decision-making approach
- Cross-team relationship building
- Bias toward frequent deployments and real-world feedback

## Company Benefits
Comprehensive package including 401(k) matching, health/dental/vision insurance, flexible PTO, parental leave, home office stipends, mental health benefits.
