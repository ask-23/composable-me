# Lead Platform Engineer – Bookmarked

**Position:** Lead Platform Engineer
**Location:** Remote (Dallas, TX preferred)
**Type:** Full-time, Senior/Leadership
**Reports to:** CTO

## Overview
Bookmarked seeks a Lead Platform Engineer to oversee technical delivery of its AI-powered library management platform. This hands-on leadership role combines coding, team mentorship, and infrastructure management.

## Key Responsibilities
- Lead internal and offshore development teams to meet timelines and quality standards
- Convert product objectives into technical tasks alongside the Product Owner
- Mentor developers while fostering accountability and ownership
- Coordinate with QA to identify and resolve platform issues
- Manage AWS infrastructure, CI/CD pipelines, and monitoring systems
- Maintain load testing automation and test coverage
- Develop across Node.js/TypeScript, React, AI/ML services, and data pipelines

## Time Allocation
- Hands-on coding & code reviews: 40–50%
- Task planning & team leadership: 20–25%
- AWS infrastructure & DevOps: 10–15%
- QA coordination: 10–15%
- Architecture planning: 5–10%

## Required Qualifications
- 6+ years engineering experience (full-stack or platform focus)
- 2+ years technical leadership or management
- Advanced AWS and DevOps expertise
- Strong Node.js (TypeScript) and React proficiency
- Scalable systems and data pipeline experience
- Cross-timezone communication skills

## Bonus Qualifications
- K–12 systems or SIS experience
- Startup background
