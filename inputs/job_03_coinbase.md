# Engineering Manager (Institutional - Derivatives) - Coinbase

**Position:** Engineering Manager (Institutional - Derivatives)
**Location:** Remote (USA-based); requires in-person offsites
**Salary:** $218,025–$256,500 USD annually
**Job ID:** P74675

## Position Overview
This role leads engineering teams building mission-critical derivatives execution systems for institutional crypto products at Coinbase's Institutional Product Group.

## Key Responsibilities
- Lead the engineering teams responsible for building the mission critical systems powering institutional products
- Translate vision into roadmaps with engineers, designers, product managers, and leadership
- Provide architectural guidance while maintaining operational excellence for distributed systems
- Coach direct reports and support career advancement
- Partner with talent teams to hire aligned engineers
- Drive engineering quality standards and SLA compliance

## Required Qualifications
- Minimum 7 years software engineering experience
- At least 2 years in engineering management
- Strategic and tactical planning balance
- Experience in creating, delivering, and operating multi-tenanted, distributed systems at scale
- Hands-on capability in code review, technical documentation, incident response, and debugging
- Passion for open financial systems

## Preferred Qualifications
- Trading background
- Experience in derivatives (futures, perps, options, prediction markets)
