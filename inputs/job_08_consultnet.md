# Platform Engineering Lead - Kubernetes & Observability - ConsultNet

**Position:** Platform Engineering Lead - Kubernetes & Observability
**Location:** Remote (U.S.) | Jacksonville, FL
**Company:** ConsultNet, LLC
**Type:** Full-time

## Overview
This role seeks a hands-on engineering leader to oversee cloud platform evolution and observability systems. The ideal candidate combines senior engineering expertise with people leadership skills, ready to guide a team focused on delivering scalable, reliable, and cost-efficient container platforms.

## Key Responsibilities
- Lead and mentor teams managing Kubernetes (EKS), observability services, and developer tools
- Drive modernization of container infrastructure and eliminate on-premises dependencies
- Manage observability platforms including logging, tracing, and metrics solutions
- Implement automation using Terraform, Helm, and Infrastructure-as-Code frameworks
- Optimize platform scalability and cloud spending through resource governance
- Partner with development teams to enhance software delivery
- Define and track operational health metrics to drive continuous improvement
- Champion DevOps and SRE best practices

## Required Qualifications
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or equivalent experience
- 10+ years in infrastructure, systems engineering, or DevOps roles
- 3+ years in technical lead or people-management capacity
- Enterprise-scale Kubernetes (EKS) experience
- AWS expertise (VPC, IAM, EC2, EKS), Terraform, Helm, Linux administration
- Proficiency with observability tools such as Splunk, Prometheus, Grafana, OpenTelemetry, New Relic, or Cribl
- Demonstrated mentoring and team coaching abilities
- Strong communication bridging infrastructure and application teams

## Preferred Experience
- Legacy workload migration to containerized architectures
- FinOps and cost-optimization initiatives
- Service mesh frameworks (Istio, Linkerd)
- Python or JavaScript scripting
- SLO/SLI and reliability engineering knowledge
