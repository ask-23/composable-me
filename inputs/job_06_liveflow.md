# Engineering Manager - LiveFlow

**Position:** Engineering Manager
**Location:** Fully Remote
**Company:** LiveFlow (fintech, YC-backed, $21M raised)

## About the Company
LiveFlow is a fintech platform enabling lean finance teams to manage large enterprises. Founded by former Revolut team members, the company has raised $21M from top-tier investors including Y Combinator and has thousands of customers.

## Position Overview
LiveFlow seeks a servant-leader engineering manager to handle day-to-day operations while the CTO focuses on vision and external representation. The company has outgrown their flat engineering org chart and needs operational leadership.

## Key Responsibilities
- People Management: Conduct 1:1s, provide feedback, manage reviews, and foster growth
- Execution: Ensure teams reach ambitious goals efficiently
- Hiring & Scaling: Own recruitment pipeline and onboarding processes
- Cross-functional Alignment: Coordinate with Product, Design, and business teams
- Technical Excellence: Manage observability, on-call rotations, CI/CD, incident response, and technical debt

## Required Qualifications
- 3+ years leading 8-20 engineers, including remote teams
- Proven ability to hire, coach, and retain talent
- Player-coach comfortable with code review and technical design
- Demonstrated success launching new products and scaling features

## Preferred Qualifications
- Experience growing teams from Series B/C to 30-40 engineers
- Expertise in Elixir, BEAM, or functional programming
- Fintech or finance domain experience
- Community presence and reputation

## Benefits
Competitive salary, equity, health insurance, pension plan, equipment, learning budget, and unlimited vacation.
